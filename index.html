I have experience in C++, Python, HTML, CSS, and javaScript.

I'm ambitious. I've got big dreams and big ideas when it comes to applying what I'm learning here. The front-to-back arsenal given by this course provides a variety of skills that are applicable well beyond the bounds of just web development.

For now, however, I am still learning - and I don't plan on stopping anytime soon. Checkout what I've got so far here.

WHERE WORDS FAIL, MUSIC SPEAKS

You could say that music has been instrumental in making me who I am today (bah-dum tsssssss). I play any instrument I can get my hands on, I compose, write lyrics, record, and produce.

I've produced several pieces to-order for clients spanning from advertisements to indie horror films.

Personal Projects

PLACEMENT IS KEY

Rabi Nepal

The Rabi Nepal clothing line was launched in January of 2018 as a collaborative effort between RABI Clothing Imports and PCLee. The line is comprised entirely of nepal-made clothing, imported directly from a family-run factory outside Kathmandu. My involvement in this project started out with location scouting, but I eventually took point on jumpstarting a branding and marketing strategy.

American Bar Brand

ABB (American Bar Brand) is a pet project and business experiment I started in early 2018. The company retails and wholesales drinkware products, geared somewhat exclusively towards bar products. The experiement was a success; our number one selling product was the ABB metal straw, of which we sold just over 2,500 in just under three months. American Bar Brand is currently 'on hold' until the product line is completely put together
